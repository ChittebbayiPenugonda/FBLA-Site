About ASA

The Asian Student Association (ASA) at South Forsyth aims to create a diverse and welcoming community for Asian Students within the campus. ASA organizes various cultural, political-educational, and social events. We seek to promote a cultural and political awareness of Asian traditions and diversity in our community. We welcome any student who is interested in participating in the association's activities to help us achieve our mission and objectives.
